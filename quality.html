How to Identify Pure Spices

We believe an educated customer is our best customer.

Adulteration is common in the spice market. Here's how you can tell Tawabil spices apart from the rest:

Pure Authentic Cardamom

Plump, green pods that feel heavy. When opened, seeds appear dark brown/black and are sticky with oil.

Pure Real Cinnamon

Tan brown color, thin multiple layers like a cigar, easily breakable, and has a sweet, delicate aroma.

Pure Black Pepper

Uniform dark brown/black color with a wrinkled surface. Should sink in water (heavy), not float (light/hollow).
